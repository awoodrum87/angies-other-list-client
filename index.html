About Angie's Other List

On Angie's Other List customer service providers get to review their customers! In 2005 while working part time at a local restaurant Angie, still an angsty and mouthy teenager, had many discussions with her manager about being polite to customers even when they're mean. Angie survived this, and a few more years of customer service work, by knowing one day she would get her chance for revenge! Do you serve a bad tipper that needs to be called out? Know someone that straight up lies about their service get free food? Join Angie's Other List to put them on blast!
